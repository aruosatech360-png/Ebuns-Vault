UGC Handbook — How to Start Getting Paid for Your Content

Published October 3, 2025 by Ebun's Vault

Short-form videos and authentic product stories are the currency of modern brand marketing. But how do you go from posting a few clips to getting paid? This post summarizes the key lessons from our free UGC Handbook and gives you simple, actionable steps to start earning.

Why brands pay for UGC

Brands want authenticity. UGC creators provide real-life use-cases and relatable stories that perform better than staged ads. The better your storytelling and speed of delivery, the more valuable you become.

3 quick ways to get started today

  • Create 3 short videos showing a single product used naturally (15–30s).
  • Pick 10 small brands or local businesses and send a brief pitch using our template.
  • Offer a paid test: 1 video at a small fee to prove your value.

What you’ll find in the handbook

The free PDF includes pitch templates, pricing examples, a list of beginner-friendly brands, and shortcuts for filming and editing that save time without sacrificing quality.

“Start with value, not expectations. If your content helps the brand sell, the money follows.”

A simple pitch template (copy-paste)

Hi [Brand Name],

I love your [product]. I make short, authentic videos that help brands show real customers how products are used.

I’d love to create one 15–30s video for you as a paid test. My rate for a simple video is [₦ / $]. If you’re open, I can send examples and a quick plan.

Thanks for considering — Ebun

Use that template, personalize one line, and send it to 10 brands this week. Track replies and follow up once after 5 days.
